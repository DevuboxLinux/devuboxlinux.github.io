Midweight & Fast

Optimized Openbox environment running on ~600MB RAM. Breaths new life into older hardware like Core 2 Duo machines.

Secure Core

Built on the rock-solid foundation of Devuan and MX Linux. Enjoy Debian stability without the systemd complexity.

Preloaded Arsenal

Ready out-of-the-box with LibreOffice, GIMP, OBS Studio, and multimedia codecs. No post-install hunting required.

Broad Compatibility

Includes non-free firmware and Advanced Hardware Support (AHS) kernels. Works on both modern rigs and legacy laptops.

Community Driven
